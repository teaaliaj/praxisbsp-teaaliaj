Spilling the Tea on Travel

Have you ever planned a trip on a city you have never visited before and all you have found online was just too touristy for you? Don't get me wrong there is nothing wrong with main tourist attractions but sometimes the soul needs a local experience rather that something you could find while scrolling on TikTok or Instagram, and there is when The travel tea comes to help. We offer findings that give that perfect mix of fancy rooftop bars but also immaculate street food in a hidden alley.

Whether you want the perfect "Instagram" spots or the hidden gems that you can only find by asking locals, (sometimes can't even be found on Google Maps) I'm here to spill ALL the tea with you 😉.
