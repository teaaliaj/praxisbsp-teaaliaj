The story and Mission

The travel tea was founded by Tea Aliaj, a TU Wien student and a travel enthusiast. Her idea was creating a platform that features curated guides based on her personal travel experiences.Her love for authentic local food, cozy stays, unique itineraries and hidden gems is felt through her recommendations that elevate the average travel experience into a more local feeling. But because no two travelers are the same ,our Customized Travel Guides came to life, ensuring your journey is tailored to your preferences. The travel tea is more than just a travel platform, it is a space designed to make traveling exciting again and connect you with undiscovered cultural experiences.
